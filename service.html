Our Process

Our process includes 4 phases: Consultation, Design & Approval, Installation and Maintenance. Whether you’re looking for a complex live wall or simple potted plants for your property, we'll guide you from concept to completion. Leasing packages are also available.

CONSULTATION

Our interior landscape specialists are on hand to provide the most up-to-date, industry information. Guidance on plant selection, with speciﬁc attention to light requirements, along with information on soil, fertilizer and watering/drainage techniques, will leave you assured in the service we provide, and knowledgeable on the product you are investing in.

DESIGN & APPROVAL

With the use of photo imaging, our design team will create a interior landscape that places considerable signiﬁcance on aesthetics, light conducive placement, & budget. We then provide you with an estimate for the recommended plants, and maintenance packages. We work with our customers until the perfect solution meets their plant needs.

INSTALLATION

Our team is accomplished in completing installations of varying dynamics, both on time and within budget. We have the infrastructure to complete installations with minimal fuss and maximum eﬃciency, with respect for our client, the number one priority. We also create a very simple design for our client with budget .

MAINTENANCE

We wants to see your plants thrive! To protect your plant investment, we reccommend our maintenance packages to ensure the long-term health of your plants. Depending on your ﬁnal plant solution, we will arrange our plant technicians to service your plants regulary as outlined in our unique Client Care program.
